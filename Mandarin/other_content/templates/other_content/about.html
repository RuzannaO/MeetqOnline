Who We Are

Purpose

Founded in 2021 by a team of young enthusiastic Armenians, Meetq educational platform aims to provide compatriots in Armenia and around the world with the opportunities to connect and socialize through diverse educational programs. Pursuing the goal of having an inclusive platform environment, Meetq welcomes everyone of any age or knowledge level. It is our hope that the platform will become a venue for building a vibrant virtual Armenian community with impactful synergy.

Why?

Education is the driving force for innovation and development. Striving for quality and multi-focus programs, our team has come with diverse educational backgrounds and shares common concerns over the existing educational system/practices in Armenia and beyond. The Platform aims to offer programs that are based on engaging and interactive teaching methods. We are ready to put all possible efforts to build a new, unconventional, open-minded, vibrant and versatile educational environment. A core principle we follow is: Along with teaching/sharing knowledge, we’d better help the students to discover themselves.

How?

The fees have been optimized to have an inclusive community and to address all the members’ interests. And, yet, we understand that there will be some, especially in Armenia, that may not be able to afford joining our community. For this, we are aiming to build internal mechanisms to support others. One such mechanism will be the virtual Meetq points to be earned by community members. Those points are not a replacement for conventional grades, but become an improved version of them. The participants can use their grades earned by outstanding activity and achievements to buy gifts for others, take part in advanced projects, courses, and give scholarships to those who need them. A key principle is that one that wants to join will never be refused. So, to achieve the realization of this, we try to do the best to motivate our students to work harder, be creative and proactive. The virtual Meetq points will never be sold.

What?

  • Broad range of classes and courses
  • Various Tournaments (Chess, Programming, Math ...)
  • Exams & Quizzes
  • Creative Projects
  • Forums & Blogs
  • Talks & Podcasts
  • Other interesting online/offline events.

These and more to come to make a great and thriving community.
