About Us

Founded in 2021 by a team of young enthusiastic Armenians, MeetqOnline educational platform aims to provide compatriots in Armenia and around the world with the opportunities to connect and socialize through diverse educational programs. Pursuing the goal of having an inclusive platform environment, Meetq welcomes everyone of any age or knowledge level. It is our hope that the platform will become a venue for building a vibrant virtual Armenian community with impactful synergy.
